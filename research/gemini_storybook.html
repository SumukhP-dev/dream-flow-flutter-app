Google Gemini can now create AI-generated bedtime storybooks with custom art and audio

TOI Tech Desk | TIMESOFINDIA.COM | Aug 6, 2025, 09:45 IST
Google's Gemini app introduces 'Storybook,' a feature allowing us... Read More
Google's Gemini app introduces 'Storybook,' a feature allowing users to create personalized, illustrated storybooks with read-aloud narration. Users can describe any story, and Gemini generates a unique 10-page book with custom art and audio in over 45 languages. This feature is designed to revolutionize family reading time and offers creative interaction for all ages. Read Less

Google has introduced a new and innovative in its Gemini app. The Gemini app users are now allowed to create personalised, illustrated storybooks with complete read-aloud narration for bedtime stories. Called ‘Storybook’ the feature is designed to revolutionise family reading time with the help of bespoke narratives. “Today we’re announcing a new way to bring your ideas to life in the Gemini app: personalized, illustrated storybooks complete with read-aloud narration,” said Google.

“Simply describe any story you can imagine, and Gemini generates a unique 10-page book with custom art and audio. For a truly personal touch, you can ask Gemini to draw inspiration from your own photos and files. Bring your vision to life in any style imaginable: from pixel art and comics to claymation, crochet, and even coloring books, in more than 45 languages,” added the company.

Gemini app’s Storybook feature

The Storybook feature available for all the Gemini users globally allow them to simply describe any story they can imagine, and Gemini will generate a unique 10-page digital book. Users can choose from a wide range of artistic styles, including pixel art, comics, claymation, crochet, and even coloring book formats, available in over 45 languages.

Tailored for kids

Parents can use Gemini to:
* Explain complex topics in kid-friendly language
* Teach life lessons through storytelling
* Turn children’s drawings into full-fledged narratives
* Create adventures based on family photos and memories

For example, uploading a child’s drawing and prompting Gemini with “Write a story that brings this to life” results in a fully illustrated tale inspired by the artwork.
“Simply describe any story you can imagine, and Gemini generates a unique 10-page book with custom art and audio,” said Joël Yawili, Product Manager for the Gemini app.

While the feature is primarily designed for children's entertainment and education, early users have noted its appeal for all ages. The ability to customize characters, settings, and even moral lessons within the stories offers a new level of creative interaction.
