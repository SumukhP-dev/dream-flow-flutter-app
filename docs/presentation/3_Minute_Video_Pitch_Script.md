## **Technical Production Guide:**

### **Equipment Setup:**

- **Camera**: Phone/webcam at eye level, stable mount
- **Audio**: External microphone or quiet room with good acoustics
- **Lighting**: Natural light facing you, or simple ring light setup
- **Background**: Clean, professional, or branded backdrop aligned with slide design

### **Slide Integration Options:**

#### **Option 1: Split Screen (Recommended)**

- **Setup**: You on left/right, slides on opposite side
- **Software**: OBS Studio, Zoom, or similar screen recording
- **Advantage**: Audience sees both you and slides simultaneously

#### **Option 2: Slide Overlay**

- **Setup**: Full screen slides with small video overlay of you
- **Software**: PowerPoint recording, Loom, or video editing software
- **Advantage**: Slides are primary focus, you provide narration

#### **Option 3: Sequential Presentation**

- **Setup**: Alternate between you speaking and slide displays
- **Editing**: Required to sync timing with slide transitions
- **Advantage**: Clean separation, easier to record

### **Recording Workflow:**

1. **Rehearsal**: Practice with actual slides 3-5 times
2. **Test recording**: Do a 30-second test to check audio/video quality
3. **Full recording**: Multiple takes recommended
4. **Backup audio**: Record audio separately as insurance
5. **Review**: Check timing, slide sync, and audio clarity

### **File Specifications:**

- **Format**: MP4 (most compatible)
- **Resolution**: 1080p minimum (4K if possible)
- **Frame rate**: 30fps standard
- **Audio**: 48kHz, 16-bit minimum
- **Duration**: Exactly 3 minutes (or slightly under)

### **Platform Upload & Sharing:**

- **Primary**: OneDrive with "Anyone with link can view" permissions
- **Backup**: YouTube (unlisted), Vimeo Pro, Google Drive
- **File naming**: `DreamFlow_3Min_Pitch_[YourName]_[YYYY-MM-DD].mp4`
- **Thumbnail**: Create custom thumbnail matching slide design
